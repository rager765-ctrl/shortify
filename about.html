Welcome to Enly—your comprehensive toolset for custom digital branding, promotion codes, and summit event preparation.

What is Enly?

Enly is a premium, unified link management and conference utility platform. It serves as a central hub where users can shorten links, customize promotional QR codes, and design high-quality event badges.

Whether you are looking to create high-converting short links for social campaigns, generate branded vector QR codes for display assets, or prepare print-ready attendee badges for summits, Enly brings it all together in one beautiful and responsive workspace.

System Capabilities

The Enly ecosystem contains three core engines designed for high-performance client execution:

Link Shortener

Instantly shorten long destination URLs. Set custom branding aliases and track scans, clicks, and date analytics in real-time.

Promotional QR

Generate highly stylized QR codes. Customize dots, corners, colors, gradients, and upload logos with clean circular transparent masks.

Event Badges

Design beautiful summit badges. Upload photos, assign role categories (Organizer, Speaker, Delegate), and generate scan passes.

System Benefits

  • Unified Branding: Keep your digital channels aligned by pairing customized shortened aliases with matching promotional QR templates.
  • High Contrast & Anti-Aliasing: Cropped custom logos feature a solid white background circle and clean padding, ensuring readability on dark-themed materials and gradients.
  • Privacy-Focused Analytics: All short link clicks and QR scans log anonymized, hashed IP signatures to ensure compliance and security while keeping real-time metrics accurate.
  • Zero-Lag Client Processing: Dynamic image cropping and SVG badge composition are rendered entirely client-side, eliminating server loading overhead.

Help & Guidelines

Follow these best practices to get the most out of Enly:

1

URL Aliasing best practices

Keep custom short code aliases between 3 to 15 characters. Avoid spaces, slashes, or special characters. Short, descriptive tags (e.g. /summit26) increase scan rates by up to 34%.

2

Uploading custom QR Logos

For best results, upload square transparent PNG or SVG files. When utilizing dark backgrounds, ensure the "Circular crop logo" checkbox is active to give the logo an integrated contrast mask.

3

Printing Event Badges

Select your badge category (e.g. Organizer, Speaker, VIP, Sponsor, Delegate) to automatically apply color coding. Before downloading, toggle the "Simulate 3D Lanyard" checkbox off to export a flat, high-definition print asset.
